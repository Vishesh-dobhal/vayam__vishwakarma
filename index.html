How We Work

The Vayam 6-Step
Design System

A refined, transparent process that keeps you informed and in control — from the first conversation to final handover.

01

Understand Your Vision

We begin with your goals, needs, budget and timeline — building a strong creative and technical brief.

02

Evaluate the Space

Site visit, measurements and technical feasibility assessment to ground the design in reality.

03

Design with Purpose

Concept design, 3D renders and material selections — reviewed and refined collaboratively with you.

04

Transparent Cost Planning

Detailed cost estimates and milestone-based payment plans — clear and structured from the start.

05

Build with Control

Structured execution with quality control, regular updates and on-site supervision throughout.

06

Deliver & Handover

Final inspection, snagging and formal handover — your space, delivered exactly as envisioned.
